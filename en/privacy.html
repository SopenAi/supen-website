Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Sopen Net, Inc, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when using our ecosystem (Zakkur, Omudia, Senmud).

We use data to train and improve our models. Data is processed within a secure environment, and sensitive data is not shared with third parties without explicit consent, except for trusted infrastructure partners.

We apply strict security standards (CISO Grade Guardrails) to protect your data from unauthorized access.
