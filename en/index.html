AI Decision & Execution

Zakkur

Decision & Execution Brain - The Digital C-Suite

Zakkur is not just a smart assistant giving advice; it is an operating system for decisions. It targets existential corporate problems: slow decision-making, lack of tracking, and manual execution collapse under pressure. It functions as a digital executive team (CFO, CTO, CMO, COO) within a single framework.

How it works (The Pipeline):
  • 1. Read: Collects context from multiple sources (financial data, KPIs, logs, risks).
  • 2. Validate: Passes data through "Zero-Trust" filters and compliance policies before processing.
  • 3. Decide: Doesn't give a vague opinion, but generates a final option backed by alternatives (Option A/B), risks, and success conditions.
  • 4. Execute: Immediately converts the decision into actions: running tasks, sending API commands, updating settings, or launching campaigns.
  • 5. Audit: Records every step chronologically: Who decided? Why? And with what data? To ensure absolute transparency.
Solves the "speed vs security conflict" via Guardrails that prevent overreach.
AI Content Production

Omudia

Industrial Production & Publishing Arm - The Factory

Omudia shifts the concept of content from "artistic inspiration" to a "measurable operational process". It is an all-in-one factory for companies and founders (Founder-Led Marketing) solving the problem of time loss and identity dispersion across platforms.

The Factory Loop:
  • Idea → Brief: Precise definition of goal, audience, and constraints (legal/brand).
  • Generate & Adapt: Not just writing, but "adapting". Converts the same idea into an article, tweet, short video, and LinkedIn post with distinct styles for each platform.
  • Validate & Filter: Checking content against company policies to prevent unsupported claims or risks.
  • Publish & Schedule: Fully automated publishing and scheduling across all channels.
  • Measure & Optimize: Publishing is not the end. The system measures interaction (clicks, impressions, conversion) and feeds back to Zakkur to improve the next cycle.
Full integration: Zakkur decides strategy, Omudia executes and publishes.
AI-Native Social

Senmud

Human Signal Layer - Signal vs Noise

Senmud targets traditional network problems: noise, addiction, and superficial interaction. Here, AI is not a side feature, but the infrastructure helping humans build meaning.

What's inside Senmud?
  • Comprehensive Platform: Not just a page, containing posts, stories, audio rooms, and live streams.
  • AI as Co-Creator: Helps you formulate ideas, summarize long debates, translate content, and even suggest replies to refute arguments.
  • Quality Systems: Based on reputation, leaderboards, and gamification to elevate debate quality.
  • System Senses: Senmud captures what truly works and fails with the audience, feeding these "signals" to Zakkur to decide the next step.
Humans decide meaning, AI assists in construction and clarification.
Unified Identity Layer

Sopen CV

Unified Identity + Permissions + Context

Goal: No "new user" for each app. There is one user with context traveling with you. Sopen CV is the digital "passport" within the ecosystem.

Key Features:
  • Single Sign-On (SSO): One secure account for the entire ecosystem.
  • Operational Profile: Not a normal job CV, but a record reflecting your activity, progress, reputation in Senmud, and system history.
  • Context Passport: Feeds Zakkur necessary info (Who are you? What permissions do you have? What is your goal?) to enable accurate decision-making on your behalf.
  • Organization Management: Full support for company accounts, teams, and permissions (Admin, Member, Viewer).

FAQ

Is Zakkur a replacement for employees?

No, Zakkur is an "Enabler". It does not replace human leadership but equips them with precise data, fast analysis, and error-free execution, allowing employees to focus on creativity and strategy.

What is Sopen CV?

Sopen CV is not a traditional PDF resume, but a "living" file documenting your actions, decisions, and reputation within the system, giving you credibility based on "what you did" not just "what you wrote".

When is the launch?

We follow a "Continuous Deployment" policy. We will launch Modules successively as soon as they are complete and pass security tests. Sign up for the mailing list to be the first to know.
